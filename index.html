About Me

I am a dedicated researcher specializing in experimental condensed matter physics with a focus on the magnetic, dielectric, and structural properties of complex oxide materials. Currently completing my PhD at North Bengal University under the guidance of Dr. Arindam Karmakar, I have gained extensive experience in advanced synchrotron and neutron diffraction analysis techniques. My research includes investigating multiferroic, pyroelectric, and topological materials, with published work exploring impurity doping effects on magnetic and dielectric properties and the charge dynamics in novel oxide systems. Through collaborations with esteemed research facilities in Europe and the UK, I have developed a deep expertise in facility-based x-ray and neutron techniques, coupled with advanced data analysis skills. My goal is to contribute impactful insights to the field of condensed matter physics and to explore new frontiers in material properties.
